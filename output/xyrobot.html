xyRobot

This is my robot project. A self-made PCB with an AtMega2560 controls everything. The robot has a Bluetooth module, a Gameboy Camera and my RAM module. I bought the RN-KeyLCD and the RN-VN2 from Roboternetz.

The current software as well as Eagle files of the PCB are on GitHub.
